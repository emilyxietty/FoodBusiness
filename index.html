About Us

Emily's Bakery sells ingredients and recipes, shipped straight to your door. This concept began 10 years ago, when Emily moved to Canada for school. Missing home and her favourite local sweets, Emily would hop on a call with her family and everyone would bake the same Asian sweets together, just like they used to. This was the beginning of our amazing company, Emily's Bakery. Choose a baked item from our finely curated selection, and a package filled with all the ingredients and instructions needed will come right to your door!

Our Ingredients

Emily's Bakery uses high quality, organic ingredients, because we value the quality that our customers recieve. Our produce is "Canada Organic" certified, and Food Alliance certified, meaning that they were grown sustainably and ethically. Our milk prodcuts are OMAFRA organic certified and Certified Humane Raised and Handled.
